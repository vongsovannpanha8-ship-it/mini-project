Hot Latte

A hot latte blends a rich shot of espresso with silky, steamed milk. It is topped with a thin layer of velvety microfoam, perfect for pouring beautiful latte art. The result is a smooth, comforting, and mildly sweet coffee classic.

Ice Latte

An iced latte combines bold espresso with cold milk poured over ice. It skips the warm foam for a crisp, refreshing texture. It's the perfect chilled caffeine kick for a warm day.
